Overview:

&nbsp;	Play as a human protecting his bas by placing towers 

&nbsp;	and grabbing weapons to fight waves of monsters.

&nbsp; The player must survive 20 waves on 3 different levels to beat the game.

&nbsp; If the city gates are destroyed the game is over.

&nbsp; On death the player will respawn in the respawn area.

&nbsp; The player can find weapons hidden on each map along with economy building.

&nbsp; Enemies have a chance to drop health potions.

&nbsp; The player can find a repair hammer that lets them repair their towers.

&nbsp; Within the settings menu you can find a cheat menu that lets you skip to the winning wave

&nbsp; There is also 3 buttons to let you skip straight to a selected level.



Controls: 

&nbsp;	Movement: WASD

&nbsp;	R: Repair Hammer

&nbsp;	F: Health Potion

&nbsp;	E: Interact with buildings

&nbsp;	1-6: Weapon hotkeys

&nbsp;	F1-F4: Tower Hotkeys 

&nbsp;	TAB: Switch Tower Types

&nbsp;	Left click: Attack



Special Items:

&nbsp;	Repair Hammer: Repairs towers

&nbsp;	Health Potion: Heals player.

Economy Building: Players can buy upgrades to increase their income

&nbsp;	Gold Mine

&nbsp;	Lumber Mill

&nbsp;	Fishing Dock
